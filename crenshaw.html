Crenshaw

Check out Crenshaw, a new mixtape from Nipsey Hussle that saw the light of day on Tuesday, October 8th, 2013. Nipsey Hussle's movement grows stronger with each new release, and Crenshaw has further strengthened that fact. It's a solid addition to the commendable body of work Nipsey Hussle has been creating since stepping foot in the rap game, and we're definitely looking forward to what Nipsey Hussle has on deck.

These artists contributed to the project: Cobby Supreme Dom Kennedy (, Rick Ross, Cuzzy Capone, J Stone, Dubb Tai Phillips, Cobby Supreme, Dom Kennedy, Teeflii, Skeme, Sade, James Fauntleroy, James Fauntleroy (Prod by 1500 or Nothin), BH, ZRo, Slim Thug, Zeke. The following producers soundtracked the mixtape: Mike Free, The Futuristics, Cozmo, Bigg Bizz Sarah J, X Saraj J, GRy B Carr of Surf Club, Remixx, Bruce S, 9th Wonder, 1500 or Nothin The Futuristics, 1500 or Nothin, Jiggy Hendrix, Ralo, The Colleagues Sarah J.

Make sure to visit our mixtape section to stream and/or download TM3: Victory Lap, a tape released by Nipsey Hussle on Friday, August 12th, 2033. It features such cuts as Crenshaw BLVD, U See Us (Prod by Mike Free), Checc Me Out (Prod by The Futuristics).
