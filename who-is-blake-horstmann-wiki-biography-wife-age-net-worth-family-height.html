Who is Blake Horstmann? Wiki, Biography, Wife, Age, Net Worth, Family, Height

Blake Horstmann (born 22 April 1989, Age: 34 years old) is an American reality television personality, sales manager, and podcaster. He rose to fame as a contestant on the 14th season of The Bachelorette, where he was the runner-up to Becca Kufrin.

He later appeared on the sixth season of Bachelor in Paradise, where he was involved in a controversial love triangle with Caelynn Miller-Keyes and Kristina Schulman. He is currently dating Giannina Gibelli, a former star of Love Is Blind and All-Star Shore.

Early Life and Education

Horstmann was born on April 22, 1989, in Bailey, Colorado. He is the son of Shelly Horstmann and has a sister named Tori Horstmann. He grew up playing football and basketball and attended Platte Canyon High School.

He graduated from Hastings College in Nebraska in 2011 with a degree in marketing and communication studies. He played college football for four years as a wide receiver and a punt returner.

Career

After graduation, Horstmann began his career as a survey manager at the National Research Corporation in Lincoln, Nebraska, in November 2011. He was promoted to implementation specialist in April 2012 and worked there until August 2013. He then joined Coors Distributing Company in February 2014 as a merchandiser and later became an off-premise sales representative. According to his LinkedIn profile, he is still working in this role as of November 2023.

Horstmann made his television debut in 2018 as one of the 28 suitors vying for Becca Kufrin’s heart on the 14th season of The Bachelorette. He quickly became a fan favorite for his charming personality, emotional vulnerability, and romantic gestures. He made it to the final two but was ultimately rejected by Kufrin in favor of Garrett Yrigoyen. Horstmann was heartbroken by the breakup and later revealed that he suffered from depression and anxiety after the show.

In 2019, Horstmann returned to the Bachelor franchise as a cast member of the sixth season of Bachelor in Paradise. However, his reputation took a hit when it was revealed that he had hooked up with multiple women from the show before filming, including Caelynn Miller-Keyes and Kristina Schulman. He also released private text messages between him and Miller-Keyes on social media to defend himself from her accusations of ghosting and manipulating her. He left the show without finding love and faced backlash from fans and fellow contestants.

In 2020, Horstmann launched his own podcast called Behind the Rose, where he recaps the latest seasons of The Bachelor and interviews popular contestants and celebrities. He also co-hosts the podcast with Erik Bradley, a country music radio personality. The podcast has over 100 episodes as of November 2023 and covers topics such as dating, relationships, mental health, and pop culture.

In 2023, Horstmann joined the cast of All-Star Shore, a reality competition show that features stars from various shows such as Jersey Shore, Geordie Shore, Too Hot to Handle, and Love Is Blind. The show premiered on Paramount+ on September 21, 2023, and follows 14 reality stars as they live together in a villa in Colombia and compete in party-style challenges for $150,000. Horstmann also narrates the show along with Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi.
