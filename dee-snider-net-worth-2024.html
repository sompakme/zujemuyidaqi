Dee Snider’s Music Career

Snider’s journey in the music industry began in the early 1970s when he joined Twisted Sister. The band gained popularity in the 1980s with hits like “We’re Not Gonna Take It” and “I Wanna Rock”. Twisted Sister’s albums have sold millions of copies worldwide, with their most successful album, “Stay Hungry”, going multi-platinum. The band’s success has significantly contributed to Snider’s net worth.

Solo Career
