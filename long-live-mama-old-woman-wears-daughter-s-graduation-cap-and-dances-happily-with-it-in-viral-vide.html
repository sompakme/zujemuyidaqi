&quot;Long Live Mama&quot;: Old Woman Wears Daughter's Graduation Cap and Dances Happily With it in Viral Vide

  • An excited old woman who was happy about her daughter's graduation from school danced to express her joy
  • The happy old woman danced in a video posted on TikTok and which ended up making many people teary and emotional
  • As she danced with joy, her daughter joined her on stage in her graduation gown while the woman wore her cap

A mother danced happily in a viral TikTok video to express her joy after her daughter graduated from school.

She danced with a lot of energy and emotions in the video posted by Kpaulwaiswa.

The old woman left many people feeling emotional when she wore her daughter's graduation cap and danced with it.

Viral video of mum dancing after daughter's graduation

She was also clutching a bouquet of flowers in her hand. Despite her old age, her energy and emotions made the video to be very lively.

Man makes first-class degree abroad

Meanwhile, Legit.ng reported that a Nigerian man graduated with a first-class degree in the UK.

The man who is a graduate of the Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife initially finished there with a third class.

His story of going from third class to first class went viral as he flaunted his certificates online.

Some people blamed the Nigerian educational system for the man's misfortunes when he studied at home.
