Is Dominos Open On Easter

Spread the love             

Great news for pizza lovers! Are you wondering if Domino’s will be open on Easter? Yes, indeed! Domino’s is gearing up to serve its delicious pizzas on Easter Sunday, falling on April 9, 2023, from 10:30 a.m. to 12:00 a.m.

Domino’s Easter Schedule

On Easter, Domino’s will welcome customers just like any regular day. They plan to serve their tasty offerings from 10:30 a.m. to 12:00 a.m. However, please note that specific Domino’s outlets might have different opening and closing hours. Some branches might even stay closed on Easter. To make sure your preferred Domino’s is open, you can quickly check their website or give them a call.

What Easter Specials Does Domino’s Offer?

Are you curious about the special treats Domino’s has prepared for Easter? Here are the top five menu items you must try:

Domino’s Pan Pizza:

Imagine a fluffy, cheesy delight cooked in a metal pan, topped with fresh sauce, onions, and bell peppers. This pizza is loved by everyone!

Domino’s Cheese Pizza:

Who can resist the classic cheese pizza? | With a blend of various cheeses, it’s no surprise this pizza is a crowd favorite!

Domino’s Pepperoni Pizza:

If you fancy a bit of spice, Domino’s pepperoni pizza is a must-try! Loaded with smoky, spicy pepperoni, tomato sauce, and extra cheese, it’s a treat for your taste buds.

Domino’s Chicken Carbonara:

Looking for something unique? Try this fusion of pasta and pizza with chicken, onions, garlic, mushrooms, bacon, and a rich Alfredo sauce.

Domino’s Cheesy Bread:

Cheese lovers, rejoice! Indulge in the delightful combination of cheddar and mozzarella cheese in Domino’s cheesy bread.
