John Heffron Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography, How much money make?

John Heffron is a stand-up comedian from the United States. He was reared in South Lyon, Michigan, after being born in Detroit. He attended South Lyon High School and Eastern Michigan University, where he earned his bachelor’s degree. Heffron has been on Comedy Central Presents, The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, and Last Comic Standing Season 2. He competed in the third season of Last Comic Standing, finishing third overall alongside Rich Vos and runner-up in Season 2. He worked as  Danny Bonaduce’s radio sidekick on a popular Detroit morning programme, Q95-5, and provided commentary for the 2004 VH1 TV series My Coolest Years: My First Time, as well as other VH1 productions. In the autumn of 2006, he travelled alongside Charlie Murphy and Joe Rogan as part of Maxim and Bud Light’s Real Men of Comedy tour. Heffron published three CDs: Good Kid, Bad Adult (2005), The Better Half (2006), and Kid With a Cape (2007), as well as a board game called That Guy Game (2007)
