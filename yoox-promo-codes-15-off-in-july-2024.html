Does YOOX offer free shipping?

Shoppers can get YOOX free shipping on orders of at least $250. Any other order will incur a shipping fee of a minimum of $9.95. There are also upgrade options available that you can use to get expedited shipping. These cost more than standard shipping with prices that increase depending on how soon you need the order delivered.

How often does YOOX have sales?

YOOX hosts sales periodically throughout the year, often around holidays. Signing up for the newsletter is a great way to stay informed of when the next one is coming up, as well as hear about the latest YOOX promo codes and discount codes.

Does YOOX do student discounts?

Yes. Students can save on their next purchase with the YOOX student discount code. You can receive a 15% YOOX promo code when you verify your student status with Student Beans. After this, your exclusive YOOX coupon code will reach you right away. 

Does YOOX offer free returns?

YOOX provides a free shipping label for returns but we can confirm that all returns incur a $6.95 return fee. Returns and exchanges must be requested within 60 days of delivery to be eligible. If you’re not sure whether or not you can submit a return, you can always contact their customer support to find out for sure.
