Vaibhav Arora Wiki, Biography (Early Life, School & Hometown)

Vaibhav was born in Ambala on December 14, 1997. He completed his schooling here in Ambala and lived his maximum year of age here only. He remained a student of Lord Mahavir Jain Public School, Ambala Cant. But completed his high schooling at DAV Senior Secondary School, Chandigarh. He moved to Chandigarh DAV school in 2011 because it is famous for its high-level cricket academy.

There in his school days, he started showing his bowling skills and was into cricket more than his studies. But Vaibhav Arora was an intelligent student and never let his studies compromise due to his cricket love.

Getting high grades in school he went to Chandigarh University to complete BA+B.Ed. He is intelligent as he got admission with two degrees simultaneously. While pursuing education at Chandigarh University he got training from his coach Ravi Verma and has learned bowling skills in depth.

During his college years represented Himachal Pradesh at Ranji Trophy and even at Vijay Hazare Tournament.

Vaibhav Arora’s IPL Auction Price (Bowling Speed & Cricket Career)

While getting training at Chandigarh University Vaibhav was not getting high opportunities while playing for the Punjab U-19 team so he decided to join the Himachal Pradesh team. By joining the HP team at the age of 21 he got the chance to play his debut at his FC (First Class) match on 9th December 2019 in the 2019-2020 Ranji Trophy.

In his entire Ranji Tournament, he remained to succeed in getting 30 Wickets. Then he debuts in his first T-20 match playing for his Himachal Pradesh team in the 2020-2021 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy.

He grabbed 10 wickets during his Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy tournament. Then in 2022, Vaibhav Arora’s great career stats reinforce him to be listed at the IPL auction with a base price of 20 Lakh INR.

He was initially picked by KKR (Kolkata Knight Riders) with a basic bid of 20 Lakh in 2021.  But at the 2022 auction, Punjab Kings also showed his interest in Vaibhav and finally picked Vaibhav Arora at the price of 2 Crore. He has recently played two matches at IPL 2022 for his team Punjab Kings and has got two wickets.
