Jhene Aiko Immortalizes Big Sean On Her Body With New Tattoo

Jhené Aiko has taken her relationship with Big Sean to a place where only the most lovesick people dare traverse. The Trip songstress recently made sure Big Sean would always be with her, and thanks to Swedish tattoo artist Miryam “The Witchdoctor” Lumpini, Big Sean will always have a place on her arm. Aiko spent the day getting a photo-realistic portrait of Big Sean tattooed on the back of her left bicep, which finds the rapper decked out in a suit and tie, all smiles. 

Lumpini spoke about Aiko's latest ink, fondly recounting the process: "“I loved working with Jhené. She’s great. I’m so glad we came together for this concept. It was so inspiriting to collaborate on a vision so deeply rooted in love.” Ultimately, the gesture is certainly a romantic one, but there are certain perils that come with tattooing a loved one's face on your skin. Still, the move no doubt feels like a win for Big Sean, who can now say he's literally made his mark on one of the game's most talented singers. 

In recent news, Jhené Aiko's divorce with producer Dot Da Genius was finalized. The singer did not exactly look back fondly on her time with Dot, stating ""He jumped the ship a long time ago. He wants to be the victim and I'll let his bitchass assume the role. I only cheated myself. so glad to be free from all the drugs, depression and confrontation." 

With the divorce official, Big Sean and Aiko can finally enjoy their relationship without any extra baggage. And hopefully they can continue to prosper, as such a prominent tattoo would certainly be annoying to remove. Either way, congrats to Big Sean and Jhené Aiko. What do ya'll think about Jhené's latest move? Romantic gesture, potential L, or both?
