The Beast Within Chapter 36 Release Date, Spoiler, Recap, Raw Scan, Updates &amp; Latest News

The Beast Within Chapter 36:- The Beast Within is a popular webtoon by author and illustrator Ty. It tells the story of Ray and her older brother Hayes, who are driven out of their home as their household falls apart. Hoping to find shelter, they arrive at an ominous castle covered with blood-red roses located in the middle of nowhere. There, they encounter a mysterious beast who claims to be the owner of the castle and offers them a deal: he will let them stay if they agree to become his servants. However, the beast has a dark secret that will change their lives forever.

Recap of Previous Chapter 35

In the previous chapter, Ray and the beast had a romantic moment in the garden, where they confessed their feelings for each other. However, their happiness was short-lived, as they were interrupted by a loud explosion. They rushed to the castle, where they found Hayes and the other servants fighting against a group of intruders. The intruders were led by a man named Lord Byron, who claimed to be the beast’s cousin and the rightful heir of the castle.

He accused the beast of killing his father and stealing his inheritance, and demanded that he surrender the castle and Ray to him. The beast refused, and a fierce battle ensued. Ray tried to help the beast, but she was captured by Lord Byron, who revealed that he knew her true identity: she was the daughter of the witch who cursed the beast. He also revealed that Hayes was his accomplice, and that he had betrayed the beast and Ray. Ray was shocked and heartbroken by this revelation, and wondered what Lord Byron wanted from her and the beast.
