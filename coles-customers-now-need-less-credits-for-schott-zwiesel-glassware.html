Coles customers now need less credits for Schott Zwiesel glassware

Coles has reduced the amount of credit points customers need to secure selected glassware in a new promotion with Schott Zwiesel.

The supermarket giant notified customers on Friday of the move and promised to refund customers two credit points if they had already redeemed any twin packs of tall tumblers, white wine or red wine glasses before October 14.

The glassware promotion across Coles and Liquorland stores requires customers to scan their FlyBuys card to gain credits.

One glassware credit is issued to customers for every $20 they spend in a transaction and scan their Flybuys card in-store or online at Coles or Liquorland.

Once customers have enough credits, they can redeem glassware instore at Coles or from Coles Online.

The European-made Schott Zwiesel promotion features five durable and multifunctional glasses, short and tall tumblers, champagne flutes, white and red wine glasses and a decanter.
