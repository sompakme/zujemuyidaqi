Aubrey Plaza Wears Sleek White Dress with Cutouts to 2023 Met Gala

Aubrey Plaza's style streak has made it to the Met Gala red carpet.

The White Lotus star made an entrance at the event, held at the Metropolitan Museum of Art, tonight in a chic white gown with giant chest and waist cutouts by Jennifer Behr. The cutouts were embellished with thick silver bejeweled chains. The actress paired the unique floor-length gown with black leather opera gloves and diamond jewelry by EFFY Jewelry, including a bracelet, dramatic earrings, and a large ring. She had her hair up in a romantic updo, tied together with a black bow, and wore inky black eye liner and a glossy pink lip.

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

Plaza has been showing out on the red carpet, as of late. Just last week, she attended the Time100 Gala in red-hot Carolina Herrera minidress that was sexy, fun, and sophisticated all at once. The silky mini, which featured a cape overlay, hailed from the fashion house's fall/winter 2023 collection.
