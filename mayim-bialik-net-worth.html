Mayim Bialik Net Worth

Mayim Bialik is an Emmy Award-nominated actress who was born on December 12, 1975 in San Diego, CA. She is best known for her roles as the title character on the sitcom Blossom and Amy on The Big Bang Theory. She won a Young Artist Award for Best Young Actress in a Motion Picture Comedy or Fantasy in 1988 for her role in the comedy Beaches.
