Chance Probability Formula

Probability of Chance = Probability of No Scoring Rolls

Probability of Chance = All Possible Roll Scenarios - All Winning Scenarios

Probability of All Winning Scenarios = Yahtzee + 4 of a kind + Full House + Lg St . Sm St + 3 of a kind

Probability of All Winning Scenarios = 1/1,296 + 25/1,296 + 25/648 + 5/162 + 10/81 + 25/162

All Possible Roll Scenarios

All Possible Roll Scenarios = 6 faces per die for 5 dies

All Possible Roll Scenarios = 6 * 6 * 6 * 6 * 6

All Possible Roll Scenarios = 7,776

All Possible Roll Scenarios - All Winning Scenarios = 1 - 476/1,296
