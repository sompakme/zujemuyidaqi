Nick Prugo’s Early Life

Nick Prugo was born on October 20, 1991, in Los Angeles, California. He grew up in a middle-class family and attended Indian Hills High School, a continuation school for troubled teens. It was here that he met Rachel Lee, who would later become his partner in crime in the infamous “Bling Ring” burglaries.

The Bling Ring

The Bling Ring was a group of teenagers from Calabasas, California, who burglarized the homes of several celebrities from October 2008 through August 2009. The group, led by Prugo and Lee, stole approximately $3 million in cash and belongings from celebrities such as Paris Hilton, Lindsay Lohan, and Orlando Bloom.

Legal Troubles and Financial Impact

Prugo’s involvement in the Bling Ring burglaries led to his arrest in 2009. He was charged with seven counts of residential burglary and, in 2012, was sentenced to two years in prison. The legal troubles significantly impacted Prugo’s financial status, with hefty legal fees and restitution payments draining his resources.

Reality TV and Publicity

Following his release from prison, Prugo capitalized on his notoriety by appearing in reality TV shows and giving interviews to various media outlets. He was featured in the E! reality series “Pretty Wild,” which documented the lives of the Neiers sisters, who were also involved in the Bling Ring. These appearances provided Prugo with a source of income and helped him regain some of his lost wealth.

Legal Settlements

In addition to his earnings from reality TV, Prugo has also received money from legal settlements. In 2013, he sued Lifetime for defamation after the network aired a movie based on the Bling Ring burglaries. The lawsuit was settled out of court for an undisclosed amount, which likely contributed to Prugo’s net worth.
