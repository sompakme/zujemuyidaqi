Chief Keef Reveals 4 Mystery Kids In Court Injunction Over &quot;Child Support&quot;

Some legal issues find a way to the surface. That was the case in Erica Early's legal offensive against her baby father, rapper Chief Keef. According to a report, Keef could very well face jail time in the near future if he doesn't comply with $2,500-a-month child support payments as ordered by the court. So, Chief Keef presented himself in court with one thing in mind: to clear up the rubbish on his name. As it turns out, Erica Early isn't the only woman facing these issues with "Love Sosa." Chief Keef revealed that he has a total of 9 children, 4 of which are under the age of 5 years old.

In the legal documents obtained by TMZ, it was revealed that Chief Keef has fathered 4 children to 4 different women seeking their rightful allocated payments, with the court decided on a fee of $564 per month for each child. 3-year-old Kamiah, 1-year-old Khalo, and 4-year-olds Khalil and Kirsten are part of that assembly. Chief Keef may have little choice but to up his current work rate if he's to keep up with the payments. Keef claimed in court the other day that he rakes in $9k a month in revenue that he puts towards personal expenses, leaving him with very little "wiggle room" on the balance sheet.
