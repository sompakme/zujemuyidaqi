Scarlett Johansson is opening a popcorn shop

Scarlett wears Dolce & Gabbana, photographed by Alexi Lubormirski for Harper's Bazaar

Scarlett Johansson is branching out into the culinary world, opening a gourmet popcorn shop in Paris.

The Lost in Translation star will be serving up truffle, parmesan and sage flavoured popcorn when her Yummy Pop shop opens in the city's Marais district on Saturday, reports The Guardian. She even plans to serve customers herself.

The project has been described as a "labour of love" with her French husband, advertising executive Romain Dauriac, according to her spokesperson. The couple spend much of their time in Paris. 

Scarlett Johansson and her husband Romain Dauriac. Getty Images

Johansson's representative added that she was "passionate about popcorn", adding that it would all be cooked and mixed fresh on the premises, using seasonal ingredients. The couple worked with New York-based chef Will Horowitz on their menu, which will also include sea salt, olive oil, strawberries and cream, and "real Vermont maple" flavours. 

The shop will be managed full time by Dauriac's sister.
