George Beverly Shea (February 1, 1909 – April 16, 2013) was a gospel vocalist and hymn composer who was born in Canada. Because of his solos at Billy Graham Crusades and his exposure on radio, recordings, and television, Shea was dubbed “America’s favourite gospel singer” and “the first worldwide singing’star’ of the gospel world.” Shea has performed in front of more people than anybody else in history because to the popularity of Graham’s Crusades.
