Miley Cyrus Stepped Out in Short Shorts, Knee-High Boots, and a Long Coat

Who said shorts can't work in the winter? Miley Cyrus confirmed that the shorts and tights combo is back and looking better than ever with her latest outfit on Wednesday night.

In photos obtained by Daily Mail, the singer was spotted leaving the Cara Hotel in Los Angeles in an unexpected pairing of super short shorts and a long coat. A black mock turtleneck sweater and matching knee-high, crocodile-embossed leather boots complemented her outfit. She finished things off with black pantyhose, a dainty necklace, and a black handbag.

The performer has practically been the epitome of fashion as of late. After all, she was named the face of Gucci's Flora Gorgeous Gardenia parfum, a role previously taken on by stars such as Harry Styles and Dakota Johnson. And earlier this month, she attended Gucci's star-studded "Love Parade" fashion show, where she sat front row along Hollywood Boulevard to take in the fashion house's much talked about collection.

Just last month, Cyrus covered Interview magazine sans top, though, when she was wearing clothes for the shoot, they were mostly Gucci, including a pair of sheer undies that she put on full display in a designer-clad butt shot.
