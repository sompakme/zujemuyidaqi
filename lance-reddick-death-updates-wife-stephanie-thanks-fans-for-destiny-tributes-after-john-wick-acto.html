Keanu Reeves releases statement

The John Wick actor released a joint statement with the director of the movie Chad Stahelski on Friday following the news of Reddick's death.

The statement read: "We are deeply saddened and heartbroken at the loss of our beloved friend and colleague Lance Reddick," per EW.

They added: "He was the consummate professional and a joy to work with. Our love and prayers are with his wife Stephanie, his children, family, and friends. We dedicate the film to his loving memory. We will miss him dearly."
