Get News &amp; Photos On J-Lo, Singer, Dancer, Actor &amp; Mum

The singer made a name for herself in the film Selena in the nineties and then went on to star in big movies such as Maid In Manhattan, The Wedding Planner and Shades of Blue. As well as singing, dancing and acting, J-Lo has also been a judge on US TV show, American Idol. The Hustlers actress headlined the Super Bowl Halftime Show with Shakira in 2020 and has since reflected on it in her 2022 documentary, Halftime.
J-Lo is married to Ben Affleck. She has previously been married to Ojani Noa, Cris Judd and Marc Anthony, with whom she shares twins Emme and Max. She has also been romantically linked with the likes of P Diddy, Drake and Alex Rodriguez.
