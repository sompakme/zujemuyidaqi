Bitch I'm From Texas

Check out Bitch I'm From Texas , the latest from Trae Tha Truth which features a guest appearance from Z-Ro, Kirko Bangz, Bun B, Slim Thug & Paul Wall on the assist. The track was released on Thursday, August 9th, 2012. Trae Tha Truth's chances in this game are improving with each new release, and Bitch I'm From Texas is no exception - quite the opposite, in fact. It's a nice addition to the impressive catalogue Trae Tha Truth has been building over the years. We're definitely anticipating the next move.

Is this a good combination? How do you feel about the production here?
