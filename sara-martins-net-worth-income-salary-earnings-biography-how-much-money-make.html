Sara Martins (born August 19, 1977 in Faro, Portugal) is a French-Portuguese actress who has worked in both France and Portugal. Throughout France, she is well-known for her appearances on television, and also for her work in film and theatre. In 2011, she starred as Detective Sergeant Camille Bordey in the joint British-French crime comedy-drama Death in Paradise, which was filmed in Guadeloupe, a French overseas department, in the first and second series, and in the third series, alongside Ben Miller and Kris Marshall. Death in Paradise is set in Guadeloupe, a French overseas department.
