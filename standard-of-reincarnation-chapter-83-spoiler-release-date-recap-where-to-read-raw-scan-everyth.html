Plotlines and Story of Standard of Reincarnation

Standard of Reincarnation is a fantasy webtoon that follows the story of Daven, a former martial soldier who was betrayed by his family and reincarnated as a new person. He uses his new identity and his old memories to find out the truth behind his death and seek revenge on his enemies.

The webtoon is set in a world where martial arts and magic are the norm, and where people are divided into different factions and clans. Daven was a member of the Samion clan, the greatest martial family in the world, but he was killed by his own relatives, who accused him of murdering his friend.

He woke up in the body of Julian, a young boy who had just awakened his martial talent. He decided to use his new identity and his old memories to find out the truth behind his death and betrayal, and to seek revenge on his enemies.
