Taylor Swift Just Wore the Coziest Gray Hoodie Dress

We've officially entered the coldest and dreariest days of winter, where getting dressed can feel like an absolute chore. But fear not, because Taylor Swift just found the perfect outfit that can take you from the sofa to the sidewalk...and back to the sofa again. 

On Thursday, the pop star stepped out in a chilly New York City for a recording session at Electric Lady Studios in Greenwich Village bundled up in two cozy layers â€” one of which was a cream-colored cashmere maxi dress from The Row that came complete with a hood for extra warmth. She threw on a long black wool coat over her luxe loungewear and accessorized with black lug-soled ankle boots, sheer black tights, and a beige suede purse. 

Like her outfit, Taylor kept her glam low-key and traded her signature red lip for coral shade while wearing her blonde hair down in loose waves with forehead fringe. 

Taylor's latest outing comes just days after she made an appearance at Arrowhead Stadium to cheer on her boyfriend, Travis Kelce, at the NFL playoffs, dressed in her game-day best. For the occasion, she wore a custom red puffer coat that was designed by fellow WAG Kristin Juszczyk (whose husband is the San Francisco 49ers fullback Kyle Juszczyk) and featured Kelce's number and name on the front, the back, and down the sleeves. She finished off the look with skinny black leather pants, combat boots, and a white beanie.
