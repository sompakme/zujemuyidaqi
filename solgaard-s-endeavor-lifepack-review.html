Solgaards Endeavor LifePack Review

Scouted selects products independently. If you purchase something from our posts, we may earn a small commission.

With many of us being able to take our 9-5 jobs with us and work wherever, let me introduce you to the best travel bag I have come across. The Lifepack Endeavour by Solgaard is by far the most innovative bag for digital nomads who don't like paying extra for bag fees. The Endeavor backpack is equipped with a removable hanging closet piece that can hold a week’s worth of clothes and can be compressed into a little brick of your favorite wardrobe options (this also helps keep your clothes from getting wrinkled, by the way.)

Just as the backpackis a party in the front, it’s all business in the back thanks to its drop-proof laptop storage compartment. There is also plenty of extra room for your chargers, small electronics, or toiletries, and there are even four secret pockets for passports and credit cards. This truly is the Swiss Army Knife of backpacks.
