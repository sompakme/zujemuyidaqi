Showed Up at School Like a Star: Wizkids Son, Bolu, Warms Hearts With Breathtaking Photos

  • Bolu, the first son of Wizkid, who he had with his former lover, Shola Ogudu, has shared some photos online
  • The boy was well dressed as he attended his school party and posted how he rocked his outfit to the occasion
  • In the caption of the post, he said he showed up like a star to the event, adding that it was lit

Bolu, the lookalike son of Ayodeji Balogun, professionally known as Wizkid, is growing fast and following his father's footsteps with his fashion sense.

The 12-year-old child, who dotes on his younger siblings, took to social media to show off what he wore for his school party.

He posted different photos of his looks on his Instagram page. He wore sky-blue jean trousers, a jacket, a white T-shirt, and a designer belt. He accessorised with a yellow scarf and yellow and blue sneakers.
