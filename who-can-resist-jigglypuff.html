What Pokémon are unaffected by sing?

Pokémon with Insomnia, Vital Spirit or Soundproof as their Ability are unaffected by Sing. Sing can be used as the first move of a Pokémon Contest combination, causing Perish Song and Refresh to score double the normal appeal if used in the next turn.

Who does Jigglypuff evolve into?

Known as the Balloon Pokémon, Jigglypuff evolves from Igglybuff when it reaches a certain point of happiness, and evolves into Wigglytuff when exposed to a Moon Stone. Its English name is a combination of the words “jiggly” and “puff”, intended to relate to its jelly-like appearance.
