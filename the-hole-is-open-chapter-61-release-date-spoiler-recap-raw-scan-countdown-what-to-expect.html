Plotline

The plot of The Hole is Open follows Ja-Kang, who inherits his grandfather’s store and discovers a secret “hole” in the secret room. He also learns that his grandfather was involved in a game with the king of the hole world, who has a mysterious connection to Ja-Kang. Ja-Kang decides to enter the hole world and find out the truth behind his grandfather’s past and the king’s intentions.
