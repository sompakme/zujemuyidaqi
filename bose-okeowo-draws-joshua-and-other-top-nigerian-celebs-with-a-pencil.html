Bose Okeowo draws Joshua and other top Nigerian celebs with a pencil

- Bose Okeowo displays a beautiful work of art of Anthony Joshua

- The 26-year-old is physically challenged and confined to a wheelchair

- The Ekiti state born also has other drawings of Nigerian celebrities

Bose Okeowo's disability from childhood did not in any way deter her from maximizing her potentials as an artist.

The Ekiti state born has developed herself into a world-class artist, creating beautiful designs of top Nigerian celebrities with just her pencil.

The 26-year-old whose rare medical condition started from her childhood has an array of celebrity drawings including Anthony Joshua, Davido, Governor Kayode Fayemi, Dj Cuppy and a host of others.

Despite being confined to the wheelchair for most parts of her life, the fourth child out of eight also has the ability to communicate in the English language and can solve Mathematics.

In her words, Bose said: “I have a very inquisitive personality which is always willing to learn and to acquire more knowledge”

“I fell in love with painting since I was young but the year 2010 was when I discovered I can truly paint and draw very well. At first, I was using crayon and watercolor to paint abstract and nature in my book and when my parents and siblings saw that I really have the talents, they support me and encourage me,” quoted from Brand Crunch.

Her latest drawing was that of the boxing heavyweight champion of the world Joshua who currently holds the IBF, IBO, WBA and WBO belts.
