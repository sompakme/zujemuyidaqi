Pastor Jack Hayford Wiki, Biography, Net Worth, Age, Career, Relationship,Family

  • Pastor Jack Hayford was a prominent American author, Pentecostal minister, and Chancellor Emeritus of The King’s University.
  • He served as the senior pastor of The Church On The Way in Van Nuys and was the 4th President of the International Church of the Foursquare Gospel.
  • Jack Hayford was widely recognized for his involvement in the Promise Keepers movement and his prolific contributions as an author and songwriter, with over 600 hymns and choruses in his catalog.
  • He is particularly known for writing the popular 1978 hymn “Majesty,” which remains one of the top 100 contemporary hymns and is sung in churches worldwide.

Wiki and Biography:

  • Jack Williams Hayford was born on 25 June 1934 in Los Angeles, California, United States.
  • He was a devoted Christian, well-educated, and attended Oakland Technical High School.
  • After graduating in 1952, he furthered his education at Life Pacific University, where he earned his first bachelor’s degree in 1956.
  • Later, he pursued his second bachelor’s degree at Azusa Pacific University and graduated in 1970.
  • Throughout his life, Jack Hayford received several honorary degrees, including a Doctor of Divinity from both L.I.F.E. and Oral Roberts University, as well as a Doctor of Literature from California Graduate School of Theology.

Family and Relationships:

  • Pastor Jack Hayford was married twice in his life. On 4 July 1954, he married Anna Marie Smith, and together they had four children: Rebecca, Jack III, Mark, and Christa.
  • Tragically, Anna Marie Smith passed away in 2017 after being diagnosed with stage 4 pancreatic cancer.
  • In 2018, Jack Hayford married his second wife, Valerie Lemire.

Parents:

  • Jack Hayford’s father, also named Jack Hayford, served in the military when he was young and later worked as a switchman for the Southern Pacific Railroad.
  • His mother, Anita Dolores, was a Bible teacher who spoke at interdenominational women’s classes and Women’s Aglow Fellowship.

Passing Away:

  • On 8 January 2023, Jack Hayford passed away at the age of 88 in his home in Los Angeles.
