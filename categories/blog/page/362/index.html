What is the fear of social media called?

published on July 17, 2024
Social anxiety disorder is an intense, persistent fear of being watched and judged by others. This fear can affect work, school, and other daily activities. It can even make it hard to make and keep friends. The good news is social anxiety disorder is treatable. What is the phobia of social media? Visiobibliophobia is an anxiety disorder that was first described by neuroscientist Justin Moretto. The disease was first outlined in an attempt to properly describe a growing fear of the popular social networking site Facebook but includes social anxiety no matter where you are posting on social media.
