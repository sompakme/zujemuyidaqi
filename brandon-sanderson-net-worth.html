Brandon Sanderson Net Worth

Brandon Sanderson is an American fantasy author born on December 19, 1975 in Lincoln, NE. He is best known for completing Robert Jordan's epic fantasy novel series Wheel of Time, as well as writing the Mistborn series and the 2010 novel The Way of Kings. He has become a prominent figure in the fantasy genre and has won numerous awards for his work.

About

Known for finishing Robert Jordan's epic fantasy novel series Wheel of Time. He also wrote the Mistborn series and the 2010 novel The Way of Kings.

Family Life
