Its the Architecture for Us: Alicia Keys Shares Stunning Photos of Her Art-Filled Oceanside Mansi

  • Alicia Keys and her hip-hop producing hubby Swizz Beatz have opened doors to their stunning California mansion
  • The songstress took to social media to share four pics of the art-filled crib situated in the mountains north of San Diego
  • Keys and her boo Swizz described the house as a dream come true for them while expressing gratitude for the journey so far

The peers of Alicia Keys and her husband Swizz Beatz in the entertainment space applauded the musicians for owning the breathtaking home they call "Dreamland".

The posh house is filled with art pieces from black artists, has a huge swimming pool and a recording studio. According to CNN, the house is overlooking the Pacific Ocean and their yard is filled with a fleet of Ferraris.
