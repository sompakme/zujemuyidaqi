What Happened To Cody Rhodes?

Cody Rhodes, born Cody Garrett Runnels, is a professional wrestler and actor who has become a prominent figure in the wrestling industry. His career has seen many twists and turns, from his early days in WWE to his departure, ventures into the independent circuit, and his role in the creation of All Elite Wrestling (AEW). This article delves into the journey of Cody Rhodes, exploring the various stages of his career, his achievements, and the challenges he has faced.

Early Career and WWE Tenure

Cody Rhodes began his wrestling career following the footsteps of his father, the legendary Dusty Rhodes, and his brother, Dustin Rhodes. He signed with WWE in 2006 and quickly made a name for himself with his charismatic personality and in-ring abilities.

  • Debut on WWE’s main roster in 2007
  • Formed successful tag teams with Ted DiBiase Jr. and later with Randy Orton as part of “Legacy”
  • Won multiple championships, including the Intercontinental and Tag Team titles

Departure from WWE

In 2016, Cody Rhodes requested his release from WWE, seeking creative freedom and new opportunities. His departure was amicable, and he set out to reinvent himself on the independent circuit.

  • Released a list of wrestlers he wanted to face, generating buzz in the wrestling community
  • Competed in various promotions, including Ring of Honor (ROH) and New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW)

Independent Circuit Success

Rhodes quickly became one of the hottest free agents in wrestling, capturing titles and headlining events around the world.

  • Won the ROH World Championship
  • Became a key member of the popular faction “The Bullet Club”
  • Helped to organize the independent wrestling event “All In” in 2018, which was a massive success

Creation of All Elite Wrestling (AEW)

The success of “All In” led to the formation of All Elite Wrestling (AEW) in 2019, with Cody Rhodes as one of the executive vice presidents and an active competitor.

  • AEW positioned itself as a major competitor to WWE
  • Rhodes became the inaugural AEW TNT Champion
  • Involved in high-profile feuds and matches, elevating the brand’s status

Injuries and Setbacks

Like many professional wrestlers, Rhodes has faced his share of injuries throughout his career. These setbacks have sometimes sidelined him, but he has consistently made strong comebacks.

  • Suffered a torn pectoral muscle in 2022
  • Underwent surgery and took time off to recover

Return to WWE

In a surprising turn of events, Cody Rhodes made his return to WWE at WrestleMania 38 in 2022, reigniting his career in the company where he first gained fame.

  • Received a warm welcome from fans and peers alike
  • Immediately entered into high-profile storylines

Impact on the Wrestling Industry

Cody Rhodes’ influence extends beyond his in-ring accomplishments. He has been a driving force in shaping the modern wrestling landscape.

  • Advocated for better treatment and creative control for wrestlers
  • His work with AEW has changed the perception of what an independent wrestling promotion can achieve

Personal Life and Outside Ventures

Outside the ring, Rhodes has pursued various interests, including acting and reality television.

  • Appeared on the TV series “Arrow”
  • Featured on the reality show “Rhodes to the Top” with his wife, Brandi Rhodes

Philanthropy and Community Involvement

Rhodes is also known for his philanthropic efforts and community involvement, often using his platform to support charitable causes.

  • Participates in community outreach programs
  • Supports initiatives for children and education

Legacy and Influence

The legacy of Cody Rhodes is one of resilience, innovation, and dedication to the craft of professional wrestling.

  • Continues to inspire up-and-coming wrestlers
  • Respected by fans for his commitment to the industry

Championships and Accomplishments

Throughout his career, Cody Rhodes has accumulated numerous championships and accolades, showcasing his skill and popularity.

  • Multiple-time WWE Intercontinental Champion
  • AEW TNT Champion
  • ROH World Champion

Future Prospects

As Cody Rhodes continues his second tenure with WWE, fans are eager to see what the future holds for this versatile and talented performer.

  • Potential matchups with WWE’s top stars
  • Opportunities to further cement his legacy

FAQ Section

Why did Cody Rhodes leave WWE initially?

Cody Rhodes left WWE in 2016 due to creative differences and a desire to explore opportunities outside the company where he could have more creative control over his character and career direction.

What is Cody Rhodes’ real name?

Cody Rhodes’ real name is Cody Garrett Runnels. He has used the ring name “Cody Rhodes” as a tribute to his father, Dusty Rhodes.

Has Cody Rhodes won any championships since returning to WWE?

Since his return to WWE, Cody Rhodes has been involved in various storylines and feuds. His championship victories since returning will depend on the events that have taken place up to the knowledge cutoff date.

Conclusion

Cody Rhodes’ journey in professional wrestling is a testament to his passion, adaptability, and influence on the industry. From his early days in WWE to his groundbreaking work with AEW and his return to WWE, Rhodes has left an indelible mark on the world of wrestling. His story is one of a wrestler who was not afraid to take risks, challenge the status quo, and pave his own path to success. As his career continues to evolve, Cody Rhodes remains a figure to watch, with fans eagerly anticipating his next move in the squared circle.
