Early Life and Career Beginnings

Steve Burton was born in Indianapolis, Indiana, and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. He moved to Hollywood to pursue his acting career and landed his first major role in the soap opera “Days of Our Lives”. His performance was well-received, paving the way for a successful acting career.

Breakthrough with “General Hospital”

Burton’s breakthrough came when he was cast as Jason Morgan in the long-running soap opera “General Hospital”. His portrayal of the character earned him critical acclaim and a Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series.

Role in “The Young and the Restless”

After leaving “General Hospital”, Burton joined the cast of “The Young and the Restless” as Dylan McAvoy. His performance in this role further solidified his status as a prominent figure in the soap opera industry.

Return to “General Hospital”

In 2017, Burton made a triumphant return to “General Hospital”, reprising his role as Jason Morgan. His return was met with much fanfare and contributed significantly to his net worth.
