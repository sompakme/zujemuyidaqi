Who is Angus Young?

Angus Young, the co-founder and lead guitarist of the legendary rock band AC/DC, is a figure synonymous with high-energy rock music. Born in Glasgow, Scotland, and raised in Sydney, Australia, Young is best known for his energetic performances, distinctive schoolboy-uniform stage outfit, and powerful guitar riffs.

His work with AC/DC, characterized by its raw power and simplicity, has made a significant impact on the rock genre. Young’s signature playing style and on-stage antics have cemented him as one of the most iconic guitarists in rock history.
