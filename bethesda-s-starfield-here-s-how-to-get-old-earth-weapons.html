Bethesdas Starfield: Heres How To Get Old Earth Weapons

Ever since Bethesda announced Starfield, gamers around the world have been eagerly awaiting its release. This space RPG has generated a lot of excitement, but it hasn’t been without its share of controversies. While the game offers a stunning array of futuristic weaponry, including laser rifles and powerful blasters, there’s also something for fans of classic firearms – the ‘Old Earth Weapons.’

Discovering Old Earth Weapons in Starfield

In the vast universe of Starfield, where players can explore over 1,000 planets and wield an array of futuristic weapons, the Old Earth Weapons stand out as a nod to classic firearm enthusiasts. These weapons bring a taste of home to your interstellar adventures.

Where to Find Old Earth Weapons

If you’re keen on getting your hands on Old Earth Weapons, there are a few ways to go about it. You can find them in Gun Stores, Old Weapon Caches, or even steal them during gameplay. With so many planets to explore, Earth’s classic weapons have a dedicated fan base in the game.

Types of Old Earth Weapons and How to Acquire Them

To get the Old Earth Pump Shotgun, head to a hub like Akila City and visit the local gun shops. You might also find it in other establishments across the galaxy, such as Neon Tactical or Centaurian Arsenal in New Atlantis.

AK-47 Old Earth Assault Rifle

Known for its reliability, the AK-47 remains a popular choice among Starfield adventurers. You can purchase it from most gun stores in Starfield once you reach approximately level For a better chance of finding the AK-47 in stock, consider visiting Akila City in the Cheyenne system. If you’re feeling adventurous, you can even steal one by traveling to the Poriima system and boarding the ECS Constant. Once on board, stealthily acquire an AK-47 and other Old Earth weaponry from weapon racks in the armory.

M1911 Old Earth Pistol

Also known as the Old Earth Pistol, this reliable sidearm can be acquired as early as level 1, making it accessible right from the start of your journey. Head to the Poriima system, located to the far right of Alpha Centauri, and board the ECS Constant to find the pistol. The ship’s armory holds more treasures waiting to be discovered, guarded by oblivious sentinels, making it a thief’s paradise.

VSS Old Earth Hunting Rifle

For long-range precision, the VSS Old Earth Hunting Rifle is a top choice. It’s beloved by rifle enthusiasts and can be found in firearm stores once you reach approximately level 5 in the game. Alternatively, embark on a journey to the Poriima system, positioned to the right of Alpha Centauri. Locate the ECS Constant and board the vessel. Explore its storage section, and you’ll uncover a wealth of Old Earth firearms, including the coveted VSS, to enhance your gameplay.

Conclusion

In the vast expanse of Starfield’s universe, the inclusion of Old Earth Weapons adds a touch of nostalgia and excitement for classic firearm enthusiasts. Whether you choose to buy them, discover them in caches, or embark on daring heists to steal them, these weapons provide a unique and satisfying experience for gamers. So, as you venture into the depths of space, don’t forget to keep an eye out for these classic treasures from Old Earth. Happy gaming!
