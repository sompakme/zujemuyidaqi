Early Life And Education

Nicky Hilton is an American socialite, fashion designer, and model. She was born in New York City on October 5, 1983, but was raised in Los Angeles by her parents, Kathy Hilton and Richard Hilton.

She grew up with three siblings, Paris Hilton, Conrad Hughes Hilton, and Barron Hilton II. After attending the Convent of the Sacred Heart, she pursued a successful career in fashion and has become a well-known figure in the entertainment industry.

Career

Nicky Hilton has been a successful fashion entrepreneur for over a decade. In 2004, she launched her clothing line, followed by designing a line of handbags for Japanese company Samantha Thavasa.

She continued to expand her fashion empire by starting her second line, Nicholai, in 2007, targeting a higher price point.

In addition to her successful clothing lines, Hilton has collaborated with popular brands like Pepsi and Mattel, showcasing her talent and versatility in the fashion industry.

Nicky Hilton is a well-known businesswoman and socialite involved in various ventures. In 2005, she was the face of the Australian underwear line Antz Pantz, along with Kimberly Stewart. She has also been featured in campaigns for popular brands such as Guess and Bebe.

In addition to her modeling career, she ventured into the hotel industry, opening two Nicky O Hotels in Miami and Chicago. In 2010, she launched her jewelry line, adding another successful business to her name.

Nicky Hilton, 40, and James Rothschild have been married for over five years. They have two daughters, Lily-Grace Victoria and Theodora “Teddy” Marilyn, and a son whose name has not yet been revealed.
